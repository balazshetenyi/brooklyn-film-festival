Brooklyn Park

Free Outdoor Film Festival

August 5th - 8th

Free Entry to All
Book Now

Jennifer Viala is the founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. and is also the organizer of this year’s Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival.

"My company, "Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival" just got the approval to show movies at Brooklyn's Bridge Park this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight."

The festival is free and open for all ages. The maximum capacity of the festival is 5000 people at once however, we'd like to ask everybody to book your seats in advance through the website, so that we can hold these seats for you.
We are delighted to help you to discover awesome films.
